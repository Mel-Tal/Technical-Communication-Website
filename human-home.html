    Welcome to the Fight!

    As one of our active team members you are on the forefront of the fight against the zombie epidemic. Because this fight is not without risk, our active team members must maintain a healthy lifestyle. Using your activity monitor, you are asked to monitor your overall health, measured in HP. This statistic takes into account your daily step totals, floors climbed, hours slept, and water consumed. HP totals will be calculated on a weekly basis.

    Unfortunately, because our brave active team members are fighting in close contact with the zombies, every week we anticipate some number of them being attacked and contracting the zombie virus. If this should happen to you, we at the CDC would like to reassure you that we would take care of our team members. We will administer antidote to our team members as soon as is possible. That being said, team members must make keeping in shape a top priority, as the number of doses of antidote is limited.
